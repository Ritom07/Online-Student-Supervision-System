Assignment Submission Guidelines

General Guidelines:

  • Ensure that your assignment is properly formatted before submission.
  • Assignments must be submitted in PDF or Word format only.
  • Do not exceed the specified word count for written assignments.
  • Include your name, roll number, and subject at the top of the document.

File Naming Conventions:

  • Use the following format for naming your files: SubjectName_YourName_AssignmentNumber
  • Example: JavaProgramming_JohnDoe_Assignment1.pdf
  • Ensure there are no spaces or special characters in the file name.

Important Notes:

  • Late submissions may incur penalties based on the department policy.
  • Ensure that all sections of the assignment are included and well-organized.
  • If you face any technical issues, contact the support team immediately.

Submission Instructions:

Please follow these steps to submit your assignments:

  1. Select the appropriate subject from the drop-down menu.
  2. Upload your assignment file by clicking the "Choose File" button.
  3. Click the "Submit" button to complete the submission process.
  4. After submission, you will receive a confirmation message and email.
